Dive App

Branding and App Presentation.

A newly launched iOS app, Dive automatically captures responses to videos, photos, or texts sent by friends, automatically sharing them back with them. We wanted to capture the fun dynamics of this new form of interaction, creating a versatile and exciting identity that gave Dive the fizz they were looking for. This project included the creation of their logo and typography system, as well as application icon and loading animation, and other sales collateral.
