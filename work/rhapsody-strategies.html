Rhapsody Strategies

Marketing Collateral, Iconography

Alongside a series of brochures and banners, Rhapsody Strategies, an Ottawa-based business consulting office, approached us to produce a custom binder for their newest clients: one that would stay with them through their time with their newly appointed business coach, and would be a record of their epic journey. The collaborative effort resulted in a sleek, velvety piece that felt loved to every detail, and that clients were happy to put their signature on.
