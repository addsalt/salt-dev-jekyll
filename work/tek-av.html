Tek-AV

Branding and Web Design.

Newly-established Tek-AV is an Ottawa-based contracting service that specializes in the installation of home entertainment. We aimed to create an identity that spoke of owner Keith's love for good ole' quality: moodily dark with a rock-and-roll flair brought about by copper tones, foiled into his business cards. Just like his service, his website speaks of a totally custom experience, tailored to client's needs.
